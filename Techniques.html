Techniques

Key techniques

Adding more water to the paint will thin it out creating a more water colour effect. Adding more paint can create opaque layers to build up details.

Diffrence from Acrylic

Because goauche is a clay based paint, adding water to dry gouche on a pallete or on the painting, it can be brought back to its origanal paint like form.
