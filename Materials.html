Materials

Two types of gouche

Gouche comes in two forms, Acrylic and water colour. Acrylic Goauche will dry flat and more opaque wear as water colour will dry less opaque. I personally prefer Acrylic Gouache but it depends on the individual.

Tools

Brushes - I reccomend a good set of soft flexable brushes including a flat brush, round brush, petal brush and a fine detail brush.

Paint - In my opinion cheaper brands for gouache works just as good as the overpriced brands.

Paper- Water colour paper is reccomended since it absorbs water.

Tips

Paper will curl with lots of so I recomend taping it down with painters tape or washy tape only remove when paint is dry to avoid this.

I reccomend having 2 cups of water, one to clean your brush and one to add water to paint , so you don't taint the paint with coloured water.

Always have paper towel to clean your brush and to blot off exess water.
